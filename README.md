# Vampirism (Origins)
A datapack that adds a vampire origin that can metamorph into a bat.

<br/>

## Powers:
```diff
+ Metamorphosis: Bat Form
You can metamorph into a Bat. 
You won't be able to deal any damage while in this form.

+ Superhuman Strength
You deal more damage than an average joe in your Human Form. 
You can also perform a super-jump by sneaking for a bit, and jumping.

+ Bloody Diet
You can only eat raw foodstuffs, which also gives you blood points.

+ Life Steal
Hitting a living mob will gradually fill your blood bar. 
If the blood bar is full, you'll be healed and given absorption.


- Life is at Stake
You take more damage to wooden weapons/tools. 
(It's configurable by modifying the '#vampirism:effective_weapons' item tag)

- Photosensitivity
You burn when in contact with sunlight.
Except if you're wearing any leather armor, or in water.
```
